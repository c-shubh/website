The other day, I cleaned up my inbox, taking it from 2.5k emails down to 500.
The next day, and every day after that, I noticed LinkedIn sending 3-4 emails.
Only after cleaning up did I realize that LinkedIn has been spamming me at the
same frequency for years now!

> Throw dirt into muddy water, and it hides in the mess.  
> Throw dirt into clean water, and you see it instantly.
>
> ~ Shubh

So I decided to nip this in the bud and went to change my email preferences on
LinkedIn only to get hit in the face with this abomination of a notification
management page:

pages I have to open to make LinkedIn shut up.
