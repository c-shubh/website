Make LinkedIn Shut Up 🤫

Apr 09, 2026

The other day, I cleaned up my inbox, taking it from 2.5k emails down to 500. The next day, and every day after that, I noticed LinkedIn sending 3-4 emails. Only after cleaning up did I realize that LinkedIn has been spamming me at the same frequency for years now!

Throw dirt into muddy water, and it hides in the mess.
Throw dirt into clean water, and you see it instantly.

~ Shubh

So I decided to nip this in the bud and went to change my email preferences on LinkedIn only to get hit in the face with this abomination of a notification management page:

That’s 14 categories, each with around 5 subcategories inside, totaling 93 pages I have to open to make LinkedIn shut up.

And then they say:

yOuR nOtIfIcAtIoN sEtTiNgS aRe ReGrOuPeD aNd SiMpLiFiEd

I wonder if it was even worse before?
